The Wildcat Restaraunt

Who We Are

The Wildcat Restaurant is a family oriented restaurant. The Wildcat Restaurant was founded in 2021, and for decades we have hired students from the University of Arizona. Our student employees are dedicated to giving you the true Wildcat experience.

Location

We are located on 864 E University Blvd, between Gentle Ben's Brewing and Urban Outfitters.

Outreach

For all our years of service to our community we have been a staple name in Tucson community service. Recently we have provided food, free of charge, to the volunteers working at the University POD vaccination clinic.
